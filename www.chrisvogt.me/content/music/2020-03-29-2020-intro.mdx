Five years is a long hiatus for something that used to be daily. In my early twenties I rented a band practice room near Arizona State in Tempe and spent most of my free time in it. Then life intervened, and the equipment went quiet.

"2020 Intro" is the reentry: 13 original pieces and a single drum sample, assembled in Ableton Live. It represents me relearning the tools more than anything finished—but that's what a first track back should be.

I'm also reviving **Find Me by Midnight**, the moniker I used when I was releasing music before the break. New music will live under that name on SoundCloud.
