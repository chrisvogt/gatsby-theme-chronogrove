## About "Ebb Tide"

"Ebb Tide" is a song I've played since I was a child. My grandfather, Buddy Milton, was a professional pianist from the 1940s through the 1990s. He passed away when I was very young, but my mom always told me how she would ask him to play "Ebb Tide" because he had a way of making it sound like ocean waves rolling in and out. Although I've never found a recording of him playing it, I've always loved the song and have tried to recreate that feeling with runs up and down the chords.

## The Recording

This recording was done in one take, aiming to capture the essence of the song in the moment. It's probably the most authentic-sounding recording I've been able to achieve from a digital piano.
