August 2nd in Tempe, Arizona: I met up with Trevor Clay, a singer based in Phoenix, and we ran through a few songs from my repertoire together. No microphone, so the audio quality reflects what it was—a practice session, not a recording session.

"Lullaby of Birdland" came out of that afternoon. We're planning a proper session in December with a mic and a bit more preparation. This is what the first run sounds like before any of that.
