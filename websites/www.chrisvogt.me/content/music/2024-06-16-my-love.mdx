"My Love" by Paul and Linda McCartney entered my practice rotation in May 2024. According to Last.fm, I first listened to the Wings 2018 remaster on May 2, 2020—the song had been in my ears for four years before I sat down to learn it. That kind of familiarity either helps or makes you nervous. On this take, it helped.

This recording was done in six full takes at home on June 16, 2024, without editing. Take six was the one.

Setup:

- **Instrument**: Nord Grand
- **Microphone**: 2x sE Electronics T1
- **Audio Interface**: Universal Audio Volt 2
- **Monitors**: Yamaha HS8
- **DAW**: Ableton Live! 11
