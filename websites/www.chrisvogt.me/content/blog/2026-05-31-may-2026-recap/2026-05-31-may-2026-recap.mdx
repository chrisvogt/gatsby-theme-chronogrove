What started as a short Memorial Day weekend cruise with a friend I had not seen in years grew into extra time in Fort Lauderdale and then, instead of heading home, a flight onward to Medellin, Colombia, farther south than I had ever traveled before.

## 🎂 Birthday Month

I wanted May to feel a little more open-ended than usual, so I built the month around travel. The anchor was a birthday getaway over Memorial Day weekend: a short sailing aboard _Celebrity Reflection_ with a friend I had not seen in years, which gave the whole trip the feeling of both reunion and reset.

## 🌴 Fort Lauderdale

After the cruise, I stayed on in Fort Lauderdale for about a week instead of rushing straight back. South Florida became a decompression chamber between the ship and what came next: a softer landing, more time to wander, and a chance to let the birthday-month momentum keep going.
